About Me

My name is Justine Weslley. I am twenty years old and a third-year BSIT student at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. I see the world in colors, shapes, and letters, and I love turning the ideas in my head into something I can see on the screen. I am still learning, still discovering the little ways to make things better.

I explore coding and programming as another way to bring my ideas to life. I like trying different ways to make things work and seeing my ideas take shape. Every small step is still a step, and every little progress counts. I also pay attention to how things are arranged, so my work is neat, clear, and easy to understand.

This portfolio is a part of my story. It shows what I enjoy, what I am learning, and how I approach IT and design. It carries my curiosity, my patience, and my creativity. It tells how I experiment with new ideas and how I keep growing, little by little. I want to keep creating, discovering, and finding new ways to make my work shine as I grow in this field.
